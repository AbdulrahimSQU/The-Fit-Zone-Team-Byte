About The Fit Zone

The Fit Zone is a platform designed to help people start, maintain, and improve their fitness journey. Many beginners feel unsure about proper exercise form, how to structure workouts, or how to track progress. Our goal is to eliminate that confusion by providing clear instructional videos, workout logging tools, and simple progress tracking features, all in one place.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make fitness more accessible, understandable, and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of experience level. We focus on:

  • Teaching correct form through short, easy-to-follow instructional videos.
  • Providing a simple workout and running log for tracking progress.
  • Helping users stay motivated by showing long-term improvements.
  • Creating a clean, mobile-friendly interface for use at the gym or on the go.

About the Developers

This project was developed as part of a university web development course. We are doing it as a duo. The entire website, including design, structure, and content has been built with care to meet the course requirements and to bring the dream concept to life.
